Faq
Your Questions, Answered

What is Flokicoin?
Flokicoin is a Layer 1 blockchain that was initially launched in 2021 following Elon Musk's tweet ("Floki has arrived").

Who founded Flokicoin?
Flokicoin was originally launched by an anonymous developer, who abandoned the project shortly after its initial release at block 19,721.

When did Flokicoin's revival take place?
Flokicoin was revived in 2024 at block 19,721. Instead of starting from zero, we continued from the original 2021 chain, maintaining the history integrity.

What are Flokicoin's key features?
  • 1-minute block time
  • Auxiliary mining support
  • Halving every 210,000 blocks
  • The initial block reward is 1000 $FLC per block, halving until it stabilizes at 21 $FLC.
  • No transaction tax
  • DAO (Decentralized Autonomous Organization) governance

Is Flokicoin environmentally friendly since it uses Proof of Work (PoW) mining?
While PoW mining requires energy, Flokicoin optimizes energy use by leveraging auxiliary mining. This allows participants to mine Flokicoin alongside other chains, optimizing resource usage.

What is $LOKI ?
$LOKI is the smallest unit of a Flokicoin ($FLC)

1 $FLC = 100,000,000 $LOKI

How can I join the Flokicoin community?
You can join the Flokicoin community through our social media channels and chat groups. Get involved in governance decisions, participate in development discussions, and help define the fun through FEPs (Fun Enhancement Possibilities).

How can I contribute to Flokicoin as a developer?
As a developer, you can contribute to Flokicoin by accessing the open-source code on our Git repositories. You can submit improvements, review code, and share ideas through FIPs (Flokicoin Improvement Proposals) or community discussions.
